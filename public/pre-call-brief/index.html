Come Prepared

You don't need slides.
Just answers to these.

Thirty seconds of thinking before the call beats an hour of going in circles on it.

Where do your leads come from?

Phone, website, referral, ads? Which convert? Which ghost you?

What's eating your time?

The task you keep doing manually that should already be automated.

What does a good month look like?

Revenue number, job count, team capacity. Give me a figure.

What tools are you using?

CRM, booking, invoicing, calendar, comms — even if barely connected.

What's been tried and failed?

Agencies, platforms, systems bought and shelved. Honest only.

What does success look like in 12 months?

Not vague. Revenue, team size, hours worked. Be specific.
